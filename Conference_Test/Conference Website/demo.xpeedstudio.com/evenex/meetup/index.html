PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore

Why Attend Event

As Robotics and Automation is the key technology of the future, with applications in a wide area like aerospace, defence, atomic energy, hospitality and healthcare, it holds the potential to transform the future of manufacturing and also to open up new portholes to a world with an improved quality of life.

Previous Events

It provided ample opportunity for researchers, industrial experts, academicians and practicing engineers from all over the world to form professional networks and learn through active participation.

Current Program

The Third International Conference intends to provide a similar yet better platform for the academia and the industry worldwide to come together and to share the current knowledge and practice innovation in the field of Robotics, Automation and Sensing.
